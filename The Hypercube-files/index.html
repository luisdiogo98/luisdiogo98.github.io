The Hypercube

Designed by TheThirdMan
A set of plug-and-play collections of cards that can be mixed and matched to make a multicolored-based cube.
It consists of a total of 2000 cards divided into 32 collections.
It is recomended to use a collection from each color trio when assembling the cube.

There are many returning mechanics, some only used in a few cards.
The list of new mechanics is below:
New Mechanics:
- Plenitude — If you have all colors among permanents you control, [Effect]
- Ennoble — Create a colorless Aura Enchantment token with Enchant creature attached to that creature.
- Sword tokens — Colorless artifact equipment token named Sword with “Equipped creature gets +1/+0.” and Equip 1.
- Prophecy — As this spell resolves, exile it with N time counter(s) on it. At the beginning of your upkeep, remove a time counter. When the last is removed, put it onto the battlefield transformed. It gains haste.
- Vendetta — If you've lost life this turn, [Effect].
- Horde — When CARDNAME attacks, create a token that's a copy of it tapped and attacking. Exile it at end of combat.
- Safeguard — <cost>: Exile this permanent with a safety counter on it or return it to the battlefield from exile tapped under its owner's control if it had a safety counter on it.
- Ley — If CARDNAME is in your opening hand, you may begin the game with it on the battlefield. If you do, it becomes an enchantment and is no longer a creature.
- Reshape into <name>—<cost> — You may cast either half of this card or face down as a 2/2 creature for 3. Turn it face up on one of its forms any time for that form's reshape cost. Turn it face down any time for 1.
