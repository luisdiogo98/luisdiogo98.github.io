Mage Towers of Palarin

Designed by TheThirdMan
A plane covered in wild vegetation and mountain ranges, with small inhabited centers, marked by high towers, where the mages of Palarin reside, seeking knowledge of the known world.

A monocolored centered set, with no specific two-color themes.

General Themes:
- Monocolored matters
- Devotion
- Wizard matters

Color-specific Themes:
- Enchantments (green/WHITE/blue) — Mechanics: Constellation and Etherialize
- Artifacts (white/BLUE/black) — Mechanics: Metalcraft and Sculpt
- Spells (blue/BLACK/red) — Mechanics: Magecraft and Plagiarize
- Lands (black/RED/green) — Mechanics: Awaken and Ensoul
- Creatures (red/GREEN/white) — Mechanics: Alliance and Horde

New Mechanics:
- Etherialize <cost> — <cost>, Exile this card from your graveyard: Create a token that’s a copy of it, except it’s an enchantment and loses all other card types. Etherialize only as a sorcery.
- Sculpt — Create a token that’s a copy of up to one target creature you control with a different name, except it’s an artifact in addition to its other types and has “This creature can’t attack of block.”
- Plagiarize <cost> — You may cast this spell for its plagiarize cost. If you do, copy it. You may choose new targets for the copy.
- Ensoul X/Y—<cost> — <cost>, T: Target land you control becomes a X/Y Elemental creature with haste that’s still a land for as long as this creature remains tapped. You may choose not to untap this creature during your untap step. Ensoul only as a sorcery.
- Horde — At the beginning of combat on your turn, create a token that’s a copy of this creature. It gains haste. Exile it at end of combat.
